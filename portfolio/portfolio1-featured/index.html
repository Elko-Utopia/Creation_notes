Seth - The Untamed Scientist
Completed in 10/26/2023

Seth is a character from my original worldview The Star of Departure. He was gravely injured while fighting for peace; his face is permanently twisted, often mistaken for anger or madness, and his appearance frightens strangers. As a scientist, his way of thinking differs from ordinary logic—he approaches problems from unexpected angles, so his ways of helping often seem strange or even unsettling, yet the outcomes are consistently remarkable. Those who spend time with him are gradually drawn to his quiet sincerity and conviction, and many eventually choose to follow him.

Shargol(Discarded Draft)
Completed in 2023

Shargol is a character from my original worldview The Star of Departure. He was once a scientist from Techno-City, who was hunted down for opposing Terminus's human bio-modification program. Rescued by a woman named Duna and brought into Nabachuan, he found new purpose there. During the war, both Duna and his mentor Disong were killed, leaving Shargol with nothing and forcing him into seclusion. After the war, he founded the sanctuary "Eden", a refuge for altered humans, former slaves, and exiled elves—his attempt to create a place untouched by conflict where life could begin again. Though outwardly calm, he remains haunted by the grief of his past. Because his visual design was considered too exaggerated, this version of Shargol's appearance was temporarily shelved.

The Field Within a Circle

(Click on the picture to enlarge and view)

Completed in 2022

The Field Within a Circle is a panoramic graphite landscape created through direct observation of rural terrain. Executed as a continuous long scroll, the work captures a full 360-degree view—each end of the drawing can be seamlessly connected, forming a complete visual loop.
