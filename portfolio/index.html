Seth - The Untamed Scientist
Completed in 10/26/2023

Seth is a character from my original worldview The Star of Departure. He was gravely injured while fighting for peace; his face is permanently twisted, often mistaken for anger or madness, and his appearance frightens strangers. As a scientist, his way of thinking differs from ordinary logic—he approaches problems from unexpected angles and sustains himself through reason. He understands others’ emotions and pain but struggles to express goodwill, so his ways of helping often seem strange or even unsettling, yet the outcomes are consistently remarkable. Those who spend time with him are gradually drawn to his quiet sincerity and conviction, and many eventually choose to follow him.

The Field Within a Circle

(Click on the picture to enlarge and view)

Completed in 2022

The Field Within a Circle is a panoramic graphite landscape created through direct observation of rural terrain. Executed as a continuous long scroll, the work captures a full 360-degree view—each end of the drawing can be seamlessly connected, forming a complete visual loop.
